What's Wrong?

The Problem Statement

All around the world, millions of helpless animals have been slaughtered illegally. Many ideas have been put into effect, which include DNA analysis and poisoning, but these have only slowed the progression minimally. As the world of technology is increasing, mass production is being used more than ever. As well as providing thousands of jobs, Hunting Huntersâ€™ solution would be much more effective. Putting a chip in the body parts that would be hunted would not only protect the animal by being able to track it, even if the animal were to be killed, and the part removed, authorities would be able to track the potential killer, and put his/her aliases and the killer in prison.
